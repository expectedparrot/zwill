# Source evidence: American Trends Panel Wave 158

This tutorial excerpt comes from Pew Research Center's American Trends Panel
Wave 158, fielded in October 2024. It contains six CCPOLICY battery items asking
whether respondents favor or oppose proposals intended to reduce the effects
of global climate change.

The tutorial uses the first 100 records in zwill's normalized W158 source as a
fixed, reproducible teaching excerpt. Source response codes have been expanded
to the canonical labels `Favor` and `Oppose`; deidentified source respondent
IDs and survey weights are retained. Coded demographic fields are excluded
because their codebook is not included in this repository.

The excerpt is provided for reproducible methodological instruction and
criticism. Consult Pew Research Center's complete Wave 158 dataset,
questionnaire, methodology, and terms before conducting or publishing a
substantive analysis. Pew Research Center bears no responsibility for this
tutorial's analysis or interpretations.
